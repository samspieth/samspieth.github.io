Final Project!

This page will document my progress as as ideate and prototype my final project for HCDE 439.

Right now, I'm trying to get a MIDI input circuit working with a drum machine - it's not going well.

I have moved my A4 to a solderable breadboard to create a first prototype for the final project.
I have also ordered rigid channels to put my WS2812b RGB LED strips into.

In this A4 video, the Arduino controlling the LEDs freezes when the entire strip is turned on. I figured out that this was because
I was running 5V power into the VIN pin, which requires 7-12V to regulate down to 5.
Connecting power to the 5V pin directly works but introduces the risk of plugging in too high voltage power supply.
